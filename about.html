Our Story

From a simple idea to transforming urban mobility

RideWave was founded in 2025 with a mission to simplify mobility in India's growing cities and towns. What began as a solution to the transportation challenges in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities has now evolved into a comprehensive mobility platform serving communities across the country.

We recognized that while metropolitan areas were well-served by ride-hailing platforms, smaller cities and towns faced unique mobility challenges - unreliable service, opaque pricing, and limited options. RideWave was created to bridge this gap with technology designed specifically for India's diverse transportation needs.
